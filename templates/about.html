About Me

This is what I do.

Hi, I’m Nischal. I’m deeply passionate about artificial intelligence and machine learning, driven by curiosity and a desire to understand how intelligent systems can learn, reason, and solve real-world problems. My focus lies in research and experimentation, exploring algorithms, data patterns, and innovative approaches to create models that make a meaningful impact.

My goal is to keep learning, contribute to innovative research, and build AI systems that solve meaningful problems and push the boundaries of what machines can do.
